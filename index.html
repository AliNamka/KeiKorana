Kei-Korana
Royal Coincil

Registered NPO for Community Development

A people cannot be deprived of its land, language, culture and identity, and any such act do not bring an end to its existence.


The Korana was the victim of ethnocide in South Africa, but is seeing in the renascence taking place today the providential power of a higher authority. The leadership in the Taaibosch and Davids royal Korana families is personified in Jaftha Taaibosch-Davids, a leader whose aim it is to resurrect the Korana culture so that it can take its place in a multi-cultural South Africa.
